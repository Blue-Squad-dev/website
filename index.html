Standard Development Vs WCAG Compliant Development

While standard development may be more inexpensive in comparison, WCAG compliance is integral in building a website that emphasizes accesibility for users with disabilities. Compliance with WCAG guidelines is necessary in order to avoid potential lawsuits and/or government action when building your business.

Perceivable

Text Alternatives, Adaptable, Distinguishable

Operable

Functionality, Assistance, Purpose

Understandable

Readable, Predictable, Consistent

Compatible

Identifiable, Navigatable, Accessible
